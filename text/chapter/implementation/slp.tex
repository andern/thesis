\section{Slp}
We find a closed-form definition of the step length $\alpha_k$ by letting
$m(\alpha) = f((1-\alpha) x_k + \alpha \hat{x}_k)$ and setting
$m^\prime(\alpha) = 0$ and solving for $\alpha$:
\[
\alpha_k = \frac{
                2x_k^T H x_k
                - 2\hat{x}_k^T H x_k
                + b^T x_k - b^T \hat{x}_k
                }{
                  2\hat{x}_k^T H \hat{x}_k
                - 4\hat{x}_k^T H x_k
                + 2x_k^T H x_k
                }
\]
